Producer / Photographer

Cyprus


pan is the sobriquet of multi-culture/platform artist Panagiotis. He works with visual and or acoustical elements to create distinct objects and texts that frequently cross the lines between parody / reality / auteurship / art / pop. As a musician he performs using a selection of electronic modular sythesizers, drum machines and haptic instruments, guitars, drums and microphones which metamorphose constantly as his sets are often based on autoschediasm.
