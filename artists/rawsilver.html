Nico Stephou is a self-taught producer and DJ since 2008, he has accomplished releases on various compilations of labels like Low Income $quad (Croatia), Memory No.36 Records (U.S.A.), SEAGRAVE (U.K.) and an album release on Speaker Footage (Denmark). He is one of the two core member as well as the designer of Honest Electronics (Cyprus), XORKO (2011-2013 CY) and MJMJ Records (U.S.A.)
